7 Human Values: A Handbook on Living a Life of Purpose

  • Life happens too fast. Responsibilities jostle, dreams chase, and at times we forget to take time to think about what truly matters. But would it be that simple to make meaning a more natural part of our lives?

  • The 7 Human Values-Gratitude, Forgiveness, Love, Humility, Giving, Patience, and Truth-are not only words but also the essence of a life well-lived. They shape our character, strengthen our relationships, and provide us with the ability to wander through challenges with elegance.

  • Let's discover these seven values and how you can intertwine them into your days-one value at a time.

  • 1. Gratitude (Monday) – Start the Week with Thankfulness:

    • Mondays can be a drag, but what if we flipped the script? Instead of dreading the start of the week, what if we embraced it with gratitude?

    • Breathe. Reflect on three things that you're currently thankful for: perhaps the brew of morning coffee, the best friend checking up on you every now and then, or it's simply getting another chance at chasing dreams tomorrow. Gratitude shifts focus away from what one lacks toward how beautiful everything has already been made.

  • 2. Forgiveness Tuesday – Let It Go, Get Free:

    • We've all been hurt. Maybe a friend betrayed you, a colleague took credit for your work, or someone's words left a lasting scar. Holding onto anger feels like power, but in reality, it weighs us down.

    • Forgiveness is not about excusing what happened, but freeing you from the pain. Let go of the resentment, not for them, but for you. Imagine how much lighter your heart would feel if you stopped carrying that burden.

  • 3. Love (Wednesday) – Lead with an Open Heart:

    • Love is not just about sweeping gestures. It's in the little things, like the warmth of a smile you give a stranger, or the text to check in just because, or the way you hold space for someone who needs to be heard.

    • Midweek can be overwhelming. Just pause and ask yourself, "How can I show love today?" It may be to yourself (yes, self-love matters!), your family, or even to a stranger. Little love can make a huge difference in someone's day.

  • 4. Humility (Thursday) – Remain open to the lessons of life:

    • We live in a world that celebrates confidence, but true strength lies in humility. Being humble doesn't mean thinking less of yourself; it means being open to learning, admitting mistakes, and understanding that everyone has a story to tell.

    • Today, take some time to listen: really listen to someone. You might just be surprised by how much you learn when you put aside ego and open yourself to other people's wisdom.

  • 5. Giving (Friday) – Share Your Light with the World:

    • Giving is not just about money. It is about time, kindness, encouragement, and support. The best part? Giving doesn't just help others; it fills your soul, too.

    • Hold the door open for someone. Pay for a stranger's coffee. Offer a compliment. Every act of giving, no matter how small, sends ripples into the universe. What will you give today?

  • 6. Patience (Saturday) – Trust the Journey:

    • Ever feel like things aren’t moving fast enough? Like you’re putting in the effort but not seeing results? You’re not alone.

    • Patience is tough, especially in a world that glorifies instant gratification. But the most beautiful things—deep friendships, meaningful careers, personal growth—take time. Breathe. Trust. Keep going. Your time will come.

  • 7. Truth (Sunday) – Live Authentically:

    • Sunday is a day to reflect. Are you living according to your values? Are you speaking your truth, or are you hiding behind what's expected?

    • The truth isn't always easy, but it's liberating. Whether it's being honest with yourself about what you really want in life or having a difficult conversation with someone, lean into authenticity. The more you embrace your truth, the freer you become.

  • Your Life, Your Values:

    • These seven values are not just a list; they are a philosophy. Each day is an opportunity to embody them, grow, love, and create more profound connections with yourself and others.
